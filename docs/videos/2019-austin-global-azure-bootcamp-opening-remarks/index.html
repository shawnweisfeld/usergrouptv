2019 Austin Global Azure Bootcamp Opening Remarks

Abstract:

In this keynote Shawn will provide a quick overview of Azure, highlighting the major services that are offered and provide some practice guidance around picking the right one for your workload. With well over 100 major services in the Azure platform this is sure to be a whirl wind, so strap on your seat belt and get ready for a great 2019 Global Azure Bootcamp!
