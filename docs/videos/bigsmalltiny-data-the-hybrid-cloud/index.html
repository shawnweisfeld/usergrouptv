Big|Small|Tiny Data + The Hybrid Cloud

Abstract:

CRUX: The focus of this talk is Data (Big Data, Small Data, Tiny Data, Micro Data) –and– The Hybrid Cloud (Microsoft & non-Microsoft) Offerings available as of this point-in-time (Jun-2017). Pre-requisites required: none, other than just your interest. AUDIENCE: This talk is for anyone, irrespective of whether you are — just entering the industry, updating/expanding your skills, or just seeking answers to questions like: 1. Big Data? Small Data? Tiny Data? 2. Cloud? Hybrid Cloud? Global Hybrid Cloud? 3. Why should I care? What’s in it for me? Where do I go from here? NOTE: The presenters, Tex (20+ year technologist) & Sons (Valentino & Zane, budding technologists), are with Hybrid Decisions® Corp and are presenting on behalf of www.HCCUG.org (Houston Cloud Computing User Group)
