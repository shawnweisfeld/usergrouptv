Breakthrough with LinkedIn

Abstract:

LinkedIn has over 500 million users and most people use it only as a resume platform. What if you could leverage LinkedIn to breakthrough your career goals and aspirations? In this session, I'll teach you how women can leverage LinkedIn to build C-level relationships, gain invaluable mentorship, and create branding that goes with you no matter what job you have. We'll discuss pitfalls, quick tips and tricks, as well as a 3 point LinkedIn heath check during the session to accelerate your online branding.
