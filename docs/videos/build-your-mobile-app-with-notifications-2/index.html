Build Your Mobile App with Notifications

Abstract:

Learn how to add notifications, remote and local, to your application. Whether it’s an iOS, Android, or Windows, it’s easy to add notifications. We will take a look at using AWS Notification Service, Azure Notification Hub, and even doing your own Push Notifications with PushSharp. Push notifications are a mainstay for making a polished and well-rounded application. It's important on how you handle your notifications and display them. The wrong sound or not navigating to the item can make for a poor user experience.
