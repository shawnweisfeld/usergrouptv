Adam Kemp

Adam Kemp is a Xamarin MVP and a Principal Software Architect at National Instruments with 3 years of Xamarin experience. He has been a tech lead on several large Xamarin-based mobile applications including Data Dashboard (iOS and Android), VirtualBench (iOS), and EV3 Programming (iOS). You can find him on Twitter (@TheRealAdamKemp), his blog (blog.adamkemp.com), and on the Xamarin Forums (adamkemp).
