Lori Edwards

Lori Edwards is a Microsoft Certified SQL Server Database Administrator. She currently holds the position of Sales Engineer for SQLSentry. Prior to working with SQLSentry, she spent 10 years working as a production DBA. Between volunteering with the Program Committee coordinating with other volunteers to put the PASS Summit together, working as a remote employee over the last year and working with SQLSentry clients, she has had plenty of opportunity to hone her communication skills.
