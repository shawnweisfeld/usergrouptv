Mohammad Azam

Mohammad Azam has been developing iOS applications since 2010. He has worked as a lead mobile developer for VALIC, AIG, Schlumberger, Baker Hughes, Blinds.com and The Home Depot. He also published tons of his own apps to the App Store and was even featured by Apple for his app, Vegetable Tree. Check out his portfolio. Currently, he is teaching iOS development for The Iron Yard in Houston.
