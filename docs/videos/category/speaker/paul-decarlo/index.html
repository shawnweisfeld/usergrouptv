Paul DeCarlo

Paul DeCarlo is a Sr. Software Development Engineer for Microsoft and a Professor in the Bauer College of Business at the University of Houston. His current technology interests focus on Internet of Things, Cloud Applications, and Cross-Platform mobile app development. He is also an experienced startup founder for WinCoder LLC. On weekends Paul performs as lead vocalist in Houston's Tool Tribute band - Spiral Out (spiraloutband.com).
