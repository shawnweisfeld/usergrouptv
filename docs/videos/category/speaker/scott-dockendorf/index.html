Scott Dockendorf

Scott Dockendorf is the CTO of Remote Operations, a healthcare technology company that streamlines patient records for growing medical practices. He has over 25 years professional experience in software delivery and is a 4-time C# MVP recipient and current ASPInsider. He is active in the community, through speaking and volunteering as Program Director of the Dallas .NET User Group. This Owner of the Green Bay Packers, drummer and music fanatic is also passionate about secure coding, SOLID design and continuous learning.
