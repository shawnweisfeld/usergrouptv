Todd Stone

Todd Stone is a senior software developer at HBS Systems in Dallas, TX, where he has been building web apps for smartphones and tablets. He's active in the local .NET community as President of the DFW Connected Systems User Group, member of the South Central District Developer Guidance Council, and as a speaker at local user groups and regional events.
