Get Your Head In The Cloud

Abstract:

Cloud platforms such as Azure and AWS are still in their early adoption phase. Businesses are finding ways to use cloud technology, but the emphasis is on the built-in scalability and distributed nature of the cloud. This will evolve in the next few years as developers find new ways to use the cloud and as providers create new services.
