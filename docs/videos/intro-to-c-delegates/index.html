Intro to C# Delegates

Abstract:

In the world of Lambda, Lambda, Lambda and the Omega Mu's we need to have a solid grasp on what delegates really are and why they're used. You'll learn, in C#, how to make use of the framework provided delegates; what really occurs when you pass a parameter to a method; how to become fluent writing them; and much more.
