Quick Tips for Elegant Business Apps

Abstract:

In this session we will show some quick tips for building elegant business apps with SharePoint, PowerApps and Flow. Some of the things we will cover include: Executing a Flow from SharePoint using Column Formatting, Passing Parameters to PowerApps, Creating compelling visuals in PowerApps with SVGs, and More!

Speakers:
