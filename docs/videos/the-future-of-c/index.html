The Future of C#

Abstract:

The Future of C# With C# 8.0 just out the door and C# 9.0 already in the works, the C# team is staying busy! Mads will take you through some of the new features and the thinking behind them. Also, let’s take a look at some of the thinking for further in the future. We’ll make sure there’s plenty of time for questions and discussion!
